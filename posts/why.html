A DSL is a very specific language that aims to make encoding solutions to a certain problem domain more intuitive.

A person programs when they take any solution and “write it down”. For example a cook programs a cake by writing the recipe down. Recipes are programs and executing a recipe is the making of the cake.

If encoding made is easy then creating solutions becomes easier. Not because the problem is any easier to solve; but trying out new solutions takes little brain power and time.
